# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions
### What is MacSweep?
MacSweep is a powerful macOS cleaning and optimization tool designed to enhance system performance by removing unnecessary files and optimizing resource usage.

### Why do I need a Mac cleaner?
A Mac cleaner helps free up space, improve system speed, and maintain the overall health of your macOS environment by removing junk files, duplicates, caches, and more.

## Installation & Usage
### How do I install MacSweep?
1. Download the MacSweep installer from our official website.
2. Open the downloaded file and drag the MacSweep icon to your Applications folder.
3. Launch MacSweep from your Applications folder.

### How do I use MacSweep?
1. Open MacSweep.
2. Choose the cleaning options you want to perform (e.g., System Cleanup, Duplicate Finder).
3. Click the "Start" button to begin scanning.
4. Review the results and select items to clean.
5. Click "Clean" to remove selected items.

## Performance & Features
### What are the main features of MacSweep?
- Disk Cleanup: Frees up disk space by removing unnecessary files.
- Duplicate Finder: Locates and removes duplicate files.
- System Optimization: Enhances your Mac's performance by managing startup items and system resources.

### Can MacSweep improve my Mac's performance?
Yes, by removing junk files and optimizing system settings, MacSweep can significantly improve your Mac’s performance.

## Troubleshooting
### What should I do if MacSweep crashes?
- Ensure you have the latest version of MacSweep installed.
- Restart your Mac and try running MacSweep again.
- Contact technical support if the problem persists.

### Why doesn’t MacSweep find any files?
Make sure you have the correct permissions set for MacSweep and that you are scanning the right directories.
